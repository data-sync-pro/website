The Simplicity You Need for Manipulating Salesforce data.

A native Salesforce app that makes your migration of data secure and simple. Maintaining object relationships, data transformation, sequential execution, debugging of issues, scrambling data, and reuse of templates can never be easier.

Meet Your Needs Around Salesforce Data

Merge Orgs Data

Fully featured data transformation and run-in-batch capabilities allow moving data from any org to any org, any object to any object, and any field to any field.

Deploy Configurations

Extremely simple process makes it painless to build reusable mapping templates and deploy configurational data from one org to others as part of your DevOps/Release process.

Seed Sandboxes

Build or select your testing data once, migrate effortlessly to sandboxes as many times as needed, with sensitive information securely scrambled.

  • Secure Execution

    DSP runs strictly in your Salesforce org and connects only with orgs authorized by you – data is never shared with any other parties.

  • Powerful Transformation

    Support fully featured data transformations such as arithmetic, text & logical operations, predefined functions, and custom APEX classes.

  • Easy Relationships

    With only a few clicks, you can build reusable mappings, with relationships and proper execution order determined automatically. It’s just like magic!

  • Execute in Sequence

    A predetermined execution order will ensure that necessary data is migrated first, before migrating dependent records.

  • Reusable Templates

    Once defined, your templates can be re-used for future jobs. Clone existing mappings to quickly replicate settings and get a jumpstart on configuring your next job!

  • ReExecutable Executions

    Granular re-execution capabilities – by batch or by execution. This could save hours of time and significantly reduce complexity while migrating large volumes data.

  • Action on Demand

    Choose the specific type of action, whether it be an Upsert, Insert, Update or Delete, to suit your needs.

  • Data Scrambling

    Protect your sensitive data by using the prebuilt Randomize() and Scramble() functions to scramble Text, Number, and Date fields of data in your sandboxes.
