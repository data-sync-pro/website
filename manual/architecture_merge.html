Merge

A Merge Excutable can be used for undeleting data from the recycle bin. The Target Matching Field and its mapping must be defined, which will be used by Data Sync Pro to check the existentence against the target object. If the record already exists in the target object, DSP will perform the merge; if the record is new in the target, DSP will ignore and perform no actions.
