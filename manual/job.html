Job

A Job(pushtopics__Job__c) is a reusable template which comprises of a list of Executables, along with their Seq No., which will be executed together in sequential order. The sequential execution ensures parent data is migrated preceding child data and hence the referential integrity (relationships) can be maintained.

You can use the Job Builder to create a list of Executables, with relational fields auto-populated based on the selections on the objects' tree view, in just a few steps of configuration. Check out the Job Builder for the details.
