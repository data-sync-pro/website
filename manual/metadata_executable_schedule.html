Executable Schedule

An Executable Schedule(pushtopics__ExecutableSchedule__c) is a junction object that connects with a Schedule and an Executable. When the record is created, the Executable is scheduled to run at the times defined in the Schedule record. When the record is deleted, the schedule job will be removed from the Salesforce Apex queue.
