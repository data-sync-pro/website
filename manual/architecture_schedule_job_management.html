Schedule Job Management

Schedule Job Management is a simple, record based tool to manage your shedule jobs without any development.

Simply create a Schedule record with the frequency that is desired to run the items, and add Jobs, Executables and/or Apex Classes to the related list of the Schedule record. You can activate/deactivate scheduled jobs, change the scheduled date, time or frequencies, remove or add new scheduled items all thru the Schdule record.
