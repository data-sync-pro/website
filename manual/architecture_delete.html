Delete

A Delete Executable enables the removal of data from the target object. It requires the specification of a Target Matching Field and its mapping, which Data Sync Pro employs to ascertain the presence of the record within the target object. When a record is identified as existing in the target object, DSP will proceed with its deletion. Conversely, if the record does not exist in the target—indicating it is a new or non-existent entry—DSP will take no action. This ensures that deletion is conducted only on records that are verified to exist within the target object, maintaining precise control over data management operations.
