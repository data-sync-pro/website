Job Schedule

An Job Schedule(pushtopics__JobSchedule__c) is a junction object that connects with a Schedule and a Job. When the record is created, the Job is scheduled to run at the times defined in the Schedule record. When the record is deleted, the schedule job will be removed from the Salesforce Apex queue.
